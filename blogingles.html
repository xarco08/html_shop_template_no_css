OUR STORY

Skibidi Shop was founded in 1991 with a clear international focus and
the intention of dressing young people committed to their environment,
who live in a community and interact with each other.
Young people who dress in a relaxed way,
avoiding stereotypes and who want to feel good in what they wear.
For them, Skibidi Shop collects the latest international trends,
mixes them with influences from the streets and trendy clubs,
reinterprets them in their own style to transform them into comfortable and easy-to-wear garments.
Skibidi Shop evolves alongside its customers,
always attentive to new technologies,
social movements, and the latest artistic and musical trends.
All of this is reflected not only in its designs,
but also in its stores.
Inspired by the legendary Californian city of Palm Springs,
the offering is constantly updated.
Twice a week all stores in the world receive new merchandise.
